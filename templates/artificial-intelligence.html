Artificial Intelligence: Flappy Bird + NEAT

Uses of AI in devices like Siri and Alexa piqued my interest in the field as I began my programming journey. I thought training a Neural Network (NN) on a game I made would be great hands on experience in understanding the inner-workings of this technology.

  • pygame: screen rendering and player movement.
  • NEAT: evolving artificial neural networks.
  • Best performing NNs mutate for the next generation.
  • Since Flappy Bird is simple, NNs adapt quickly.
