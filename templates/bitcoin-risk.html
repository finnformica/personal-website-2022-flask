Bitcoin Risk Metric

  • The aim of this project was to build a tool to help me invest in Bitcoin.
  • To implement this, the Yahoo Finance API was utilised and the BTC OHLC data was stored in a pandas dataframe.
  • The volatility was then calculated and plotted alongside the BTC close.
  • The chart produced showed some interesting results; a reduction in the spikes in volatility as the asset matures and periods of low volatility generally infer a good time to buy.
  • Unfortunately, this tool is a lagging indicator due to how the volatility is calculated, and should only be used to confirm beliefs about price movement.

Period: 14 | Candles: Weekly
